Beyond meetings and mandates

Meeting overload

Too many participants. Too little notice. Would be better in-person. Could have been an email. Disorganized collaboration decreases engagement & effectiveness.

Arbitrary office policies that increase churn (& are usually ignored)

Top-down mandates don't improve performance, and they reduce employee satisfaction. You run the risk of losing your top performers.

Fraying relationships and declining culture

Lack of intentional interactions weakens relationships within teams and across functions, impacting overall productivity and morale.

Underutilized spaces

Vacant offices demoralize teams, waste resources and increase operating costs.
